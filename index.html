At **S.K. Fitness**, we manufacture commercial fitness equipment that endures decades of high-intensity use. Established in Bhopal, we supply customized gym machine systems, including Smith machines, full crossovers, multi-adjustable benches, and free-weight plates, using structural steel profiles.

We combine physical ergonomics with heavy-duty structural engineering. Every leverage curve and pulley offset is optimized to distribute linear weight loading exactly along natural muscle contraction curves. No shortcuts, no compromises.

OUR PRODUCTION CYCLE

01

Ergonomic Design

3D modeling of loading axes, pivot leverages, and pulley offsets to trace natural biomechanical curves.

02

MS Pipe Bending

Precision bending of heavy-gauge mild steel pipes to prevent wrinkling and preserve core metal integrity.

03

MIG Welding

Double pass carbon-dioxide shielding arc welding (MIG) for complete joint penetration and weld strength.

04

Powder Coating

Multistage chemical cleaning followed by electrostatic powder deposition and curing at 200°C for a scratch-proof finish.
